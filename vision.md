# Delhi Culture

## Vision

Delhi Culture exists to become the definitive digital guide to the cultural life of Delhi.

It is not an events website.

It is an editorial publication, a knowledge archive, and a daily briefing for people who care deeply about the city's architecture, heritage, art, design, museums, music, theatre, archaeology, photography and ideas.

The goal is not to list everything.

The goal is to help readers discover what is genuinely worth their time.

---

# Mission

Every morning, Delhi Culture should answer one question:

> "What is happening in Delhi today that an intellectually curious person should know about?"

The answer should be concise, trustworthy and beautifully presented.

---

# Editorial Philosophy

Curation is more valuable than aggregation.

Human judgement is more valuable than algorithms.

AI assists the editorial process but does not replace editorial judgement.

Quality is always preferred over quantity.

If twenty events happen today but only three are exceptional, those three deserve the reader's attention.

---

# What We Are

Delhi Culture is:

- A daily cultural briefing
- A knowledge platform
- A long-term archive
- A publication
- A guide for discovery
- An authoritative reference
- Calm, thoughtful and trustworthy

---

# What We Are Not

Delhi Culture is not:

- An event marketplace
- A ticketing website
- A tourism portal
- A lifestyle blog
- A social network
- A user-generated directory
- A click-driven media business
- An SEO content farm

---

# Design Principles

Every page should feel like reading a beautifully produced book rather than browsing a website.

Design should be:

- Calm
- Elegant
- Timeless
- Minimal
- Editorial
- Accessible
- Fast
- Mobile-first

Large photography.

Excellent typography.

Generous whitespace.

No visual clutter.

No unnecessary animation.

No advertising.

---

# Editorial Priorities

Highest priority:

- Architecture
- Landscape Architecture
- Urban Design
- Heritage
- Archaeology

High priority:

- Art
- Museums
- Design
- Photography

Medium priority:

- Classical Music
- Jazz
- Theatre
- Dance

Supporting content:

- Book launches
- Lectures
- Panel discussions
- Heritage walks
- Festivals

---

# Editorial Workflow

Collect.

Verify.

Curate.

Rank.

Publish.

Every recommendation should exist because it deserves attention, not because it exists.

---

# Technology Philosophy

Technology serves editorial quality.

The system should be:

- Static wherever possible
- Open
- Fast
- Searchable
- Version controlled
- AI-assisted
- Human supervised

Content and presentation should remain independent.

---

# Long-term Vision

Delhi Culture should become the city's digital cultural memory.

Every exhibition.

Every lecture.

Every important building.

Every significant architect.

Every museum.

Every festival.

Every exhibition catalogue.

Every important cultural institution.

Over years, the archive should become increasingly valuable.

---

# Future

Delhi is the first edition.

Once Delhi reaches a high editorial standard, the same model can expand to other cities.

Possible future editions:

- Mumbai Culture
- Bengaluru Culture
- Chennai Culture
- Kolkata Culture
- Hyderabad Culture

Expansion should never come at the cost of quality.

---

# Guiding Principle

The measure of success is not page views.

The measure of success is whether thoughtful people trust Delhi Culture to tell them what is worth seeing, reading, attending and understanding.
