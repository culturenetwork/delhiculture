# Delhi Culture – Design Principles

## Mission
Delhi Culture is a curated cultural publication, not an event listing website.

## What We Are
- Editorial
- Curated
- Knowledge-first
- Calm
- Timeless
- Beautifully designed

## What We Are Not
- Event marketplace
- Ticketing site
- SEO farm
- Travel blog
- Social network
- Infinite scrolling feed

## Design Principles
- Museum-quality presentation
- Excellent typography
- Generous whitespace
- Photography-led
- Minimal color palette
- Fast loading
- Mobile first
- Accessible
- No advertising
- No clickbait

## Editorial Principles
- Quality over quantity
- Human editorial judgement
- AI assists, humans decide
- Architecture and heritage are core
- Original writing where possible

## Inspiration
- Architexturez (knowledge depth)
- Stripe Press (typography)
- Cereal Magazine (photography)
- Apple (polish)
- Google Arts & Culture (discovery)

## Long-term Vision
Become the definitive digital guide to Delhi's architecture, heritage, art, design, music, theatre and ideas.
